Professional Profile
Dr. Vincenzo Bumbalo is a Medical Doctor and Analytical Psychotherapist with advanced training in analytical psychology and a Jungian oriented depth psychological approach. His clinical work integrates medical knowledge, analytical psychology, and clinical phenomenology, with a focus on adult treatment.
“He integrates medical knowledge, analytical psychology, and clinical phenomenology in his work with adults.”
He is an ECFMG recognized International Medical Graduate in the United States and a Member of the American Psychological Association (APA). His practice combines depth psychology, symbolic analysis, and a rigorous understanding of mental health, supported by extensive experience in both hospital and community settings.
His areas of expertise include psychodynamic and Jungian approaches to anxiety, depression, trauma, personality disorders, psychosomatic conditions, and symbolic–archetypal dynamics.
